This website is all about me

In this website I will walk you through my life up until this point. I was born and raised in Portland, Oregon and lived there until I moved to Provo, Utah to go to school. It was there that I decided to go on a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter day saints. I was assigned to serve in Santiago, Chile where I spent the next 18 months living. I returned home right as Covid-19 was emerging.

Several months later, I moved back to Provo to continue my schooling. I thought I wanted to persue Mechanical Engineering and so I worked as a research assistant as well as had an internship with ATL Technology in Springville. After both of these experiences, I decided that mechanical engineering was not for me. After 3 years of almost purely engineering and math classes, I pivoted and wound up in the business school.

Somewhere in between, I got married and we found a common interest in traveling. We have traveled to three continents and hae several trips planned for the coming year. We also love the Great British Baking Show and going on walks when the weather is nice.
